About

HokMob was created by one individual, Sean Odnert. A Bay Area software engineer and hockey fan, I was motivated by a lack of decent hockey info and stats websites with good user interfaces. Additionally I was inspired by one of my favorite soccer (football) websites FotMob https://www.fotmob.com/. So after doing some research and finding the publicly available NHL Stats API via https://gitlab.com/dword4/nhlapi, and using Drew Hynes' extensive documentation I created HokMob! It is still an active work in progress, but I and a few family + friends have been using it to follow the NHL, and we've found it really useful. I hope if any other hockey fans stumble across this they may find it interesting and informative as well! For any comments, requests, or suggestions feel free to reach out to me at SOSharkie@gmail.com.
